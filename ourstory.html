Company Achievements/News

Halal has supported one of the biggest tower construction companies in Uganda (SOLITON) to grow since the inception of its tower construction business in 2021. Halal has built a very reliable acquisition team in the past 2 years that has provided quality and reliable service to its customers making it a preferred acquisition company. Halal has instilled the “CAN DO” spirit amongst the teams and is hence is regarded as a problem fixer in the industry handling failed negotiations. Halal has empowered young staff to deliver under minimum supervision to its client satisfaction. Halal is compliant with URA and continues to strengthen its growth requirements. Halal has a permanent and presentable address.
